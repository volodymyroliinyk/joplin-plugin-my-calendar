# All-Day Event

This is an all-day event. It will be shown in the top section of the day or as a full bar in the calendar month grid.

```mycalendar-event
title: Family Vacation
start: 2025-07-10
end: 2025-07-20
all_day: true
color: #f1c40f
```

*Note: For all-day events, the end date is inclusive in My Calendar.*
